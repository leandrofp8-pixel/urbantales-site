FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is Urban Tales?

Urban Tales is a mobile app that helps travelers explore cities through location-based audio narrations about landmarks, monuments and historic places.

Can I explore at my own pace?

Yes. The app is designed for self-guided walking tours, so you can listen where you want, stop when you want and build your own route.

Does Urban Tales support multiple languages?

Yes. Urban Tales supports English, Latin American Spanish, Brazilian Portuguese, French and German.

What kinds of narrations are available?

You can choose between Historical, Fun Facts, Cinematic and Legends depending on the type of story you want to hear.
