Explore on foot. Listen as you go.

Turn city walks into stories worth hearing.

Urban Tales is a location-based audio guide app for travelers who want more than a map. Discover the history, legends, fun facts and atmosphere behind landmarks as you explore cities like Rome, Paris and Mexico City at your own pace.

How it works

1

Open the map

Find landmarks, monuments, churches, squares and hidden corners around you while walking through the city.

2

Discover stories

Each landmark comes with rich audio narrations about history, context and memorable details.

3

Listen while exploring

Transform a normal walk into an audio-guided experience at your own pace.

4

Choose a storytelling style

Pick Historical, Fun Facts, Cinematic or Legends depending on how you want to experience the place.

5

Generate tours

Create experiences like 7 wonders, whole-day, half-day and custom routes based on your interests.

6

Explore freely

No rigid groups, no fixed schedule and no need to follow a predefined route.

Frequently asked questions

In which cities does it work?

Urban Tales works everywhere. The app is designed to help you explore landmarks around you wherever you travel.

How much does the app cost?

You can already start using Urban Tales for free, but to have a fuller experience you can unlock cities or day passes.

What languages are available?

Urban Tales supports English, Spanish (LATAM), Brazilian Portuguese, French and German.

Is Urban Tales self-guided?

Yes. You can walk at your own pace, stop where you want and build your own route.

What kinds of narrations are available?

You can choose between Historical, Fun Facts, Cinematic and Legends depending on the type of story you want to hear.

Can I use it for half-day or full-day sightseeing?

Yes. Urban Tales supports quick stops, half-day explorations, full-day routes and custom tours.
