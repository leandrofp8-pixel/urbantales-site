Turn city walks into stories worth hearing.

Urban Tales is a location‑based audio guide app for travelers who want more than a map. Discover the history, legends, fun facts and atmosphere behind landmarks as you explore cities like Rome, Paris and Mexico City at your own pace.

How it works

1. Walk through the city

Open the app and see landmarks around you.

2. Discover stories

Each landmark includes narration with history, fun facts and legends.

3. Listen while exploring

Turn your walk into a guided experience.

4. Choose narration style

Historical, fun facts, cinematic or legends.

5. Generate tours

Create themed tours like 7 Wonders, half‑day, full‑day or custom.

6. Explore freely

Urban Tales adapts to wherever you go.

FAQ

In which cities does it work?
Urban Tales works anywhere. The app generates stories for landmarks around you.
How much does the app cost?
You can start using it for free. Unlock cities or day passes for the full experience.
What languages are available?
English, Spanish (LATAM), Portuguese (Brazil), French and German.
Is it self‑guided?
Yes. Walk at your own pace and explore what interests you.
