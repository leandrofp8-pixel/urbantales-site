Mexico City

Explore Mexico City with Urban Tales

Urban Tales helps travelers turn a walk through Mexico City into a richer, more memorable experience through self-guided audio stories.

Why Urban Tales is perfect for Mexico City

Mexico City is perfect for Urban Tales because it combines pre-Hispanic, colonial and modern layers in a way few cities do. Exploring with context makes the city even more powerful.

Instead of following a rigid tour schedule, you can move at your own pace and listen when each place matters most to you.

What you can explore

  • The Zócalo and the historic center
  • Major monuments, plazas and cultural landmarks
  • Neighborhoods where old and new stories coexist

How to use it in Mexico City

Open the app while walking, discover nearby landmarks, choose your narration style and build a route that matches your day.
