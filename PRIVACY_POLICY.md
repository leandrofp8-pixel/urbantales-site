# Privacy Policy for UrbanTales

**Last Updated: June 2025**

## Introduction

Welcome to UrbanTales ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using UrbanTales, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:
- **Email address** - Used for account identification and communication
- **Password** - Stored securely using encryption (we never store plain-text passwords)
- **Display name** (optional) - If you choose to personalize your profile

If you sign in with Google:
- We receive your Google email address and basic profile information
- We do not access your Google contacts, calendar, or other Google services

### 2. Location Data

UrbanTales is a location-based audio guide app. We collect:
- **Precise location data** - Your GPS coordinates to show nearby landmarks and trigger audio guides
- **Background location** (optional) - Only when you enable "Pocket Mode" for hands-free exploration

**Important:** 
- Location data is used solely to provide app functionality
- We do not sell or share your precise location with third parties for advertising
- You can disable location access at any time in your device settings

### 3. Usage Data

We automatically collect:
- **App interactions** - Which landmarks you visit, stories you listen to, tours you complete
- **Preferences** - Language, narration style, voice preferences
- **Device information** - Device type, operating system, app version (for troubleshooting)

### 4. Payment Information

If you make in-app purchases:
- Payment processing is handled by **Google Play** (Android) or **Apple App Store** (iOS)
- We do **not** collect or store your credit card information
- We only receive confirmation of successful purchases

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

1. **Provide our services** - Generate personalized audio guides based on your location
2. **Improve the app** - Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and fix bugs
3. **Personalize your experience** - Remember your language and style preferences
4. **Process transactions** - Manage your purchases (Day Passes, City Unlocks)
5. **Communicate with you** - Send important updates about the app or your account
6. **Ensure security** - Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

## Data Storage and Security

### Where We Store Your Data
- Your data is stored on secure cloud servers (MongoDB Atlas / Railway)
- Servers are located in the United States
- Audio files are stored temporarily and may be cached for performance

### How We Protect Your Data
- All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
- Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms (bcrypt)
- We implement access controls to limit who can access user data
- Regular security reviews and updates

### Data Retention
- Account data: Retained until you delete your account
- Location history: Not permanently stored (used only in real-time)
- Audio narrations: Cached temporarily for performance
- Usage statistics: Retained in anonymized form for analytics

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Control Your Data
You have the right to:
- **Access** your personal data stored in our systems
- **Update** your account information at any time
- **Delete** your account and associated data
- **Export** your data upon request

### Manage Permissions
You can control:
- **Location access** - Enable/disable in your device settings
- **Background location** - Only active when Pocket Mode is enabled
- **Notifications** - Manage in your device settings
- **Marketing communications** - Opt out at any time

### Delete Your Account
To delete your account and all associated data:
1. Open the app and go to Settings
2. Select "Delete Account"
3. Confirm your decision

Or email us at: **support@urbantales.net**
