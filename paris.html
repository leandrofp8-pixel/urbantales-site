Paris audio guide for self-guided walking tours

Paris is one of the best cities in the world for slow, narrative-rich walking. Urban Tales helps travelers connect iconic landmarks with the history, design choices and cultural meaning behind them.

What makes Paris a strong fit for Urban Tales

Paris combines world-famous icons with neighborhoods that reward context. A walk from the Eiffel Tower to the Seine, the Louvre or Notre-Dame becomes much richer when you understand how these places fit into the city’s history, public life and identity.

Urban Tales is ideal for travelers who want a flexible alternative to a guided group tour and still want more than quick trivia.
