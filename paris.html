Paris

Explore Paris with Urban Tales

Urban Tales helps travelers turn a walk through Paris into a richer, more memorable experience through self-guided audio stories.

Why Urban Tales is perfect for Paris

Paris works especially well with Urban Tales because the city is built for walking and every district carries its own artistic, political and architectural identity.

Instead of following a rigid tour schedule, you can move at your own pace and listen when each place matters most to you.

What you can explore

  • The Eiffel Tower and major monuments
  • Historic avenues, bridges and public squares
  • Museums, neighborhoods and landmark-rich routes

How to use it in Paris

Open the app while walking, discover nearby landmarks, choose your narration style and build a route that matches your day.
